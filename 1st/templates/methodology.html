Methodology for Risk Management

Determine Data Sources and Types of Data Needed

To provide robust risk management solutions, it is essential to determine the necessary data sources and types of data required:

  • Project Documentation: Collect all relevant project documents, including requirements, specifications, and design documents.
  • Communication Patterns: Analyze communication channels and patterns within the project teams to identify potential areas of risk.
  • Historical Project Data: Review data from similar previous projects to understand common risks and challenges faced.
  • Current Live Projects: Gather data from ongoing projects to compare and contrast with the current project's trajectory.

By leveraging these data sources, the project will be better equipped to identify, assess, and manage risks effectively.
