Data Cleaning Methodology

Data cleaning is a crucial step in preparing data for analysis by LLMs, enhancing the quality of the information used in risk identification and assessment.

Steps in Data Cleaning:

  1. Introduction:
    • Clean the data by removing any unwanted information.
    • Perform feature selection to retain only relevant features.
    • Impute missing data appropriately to ensure completeness.
    • Engage in feature scaling to maintain consistency across features.
  2. Preprocessing: We will ensure that the data fed into LLMs is of good quality by:
    • Engaging in various preprocessing techniques.
    • Validating the quality of the data continuously throughout the process.
